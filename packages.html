Musicians & Planes

MUSICIANS


Do you have a skill that a 5-Star Resort such as ours could benefit from? We might have something to barter. There is a saying: "it never hurts to ask (politely)". Please email us at info@couplesresort.ca and we will see if we can fit you in somehow. Who knows, maybe you or your spouse might think the idea is fun — and hopefully it will all be win-win-win!

Couples Resort is looking for superb musicians.

Do you play solo, with your partner, or as part of a small band? Then we may be the perfect adventure for you — depending on your availability.

Come as a couple(s), play, and indulge.

Stay 2 nights and play 1, or stay 3 nights and play 2 — all-inclusive depending on availability.

Midweek: stay 4 nights and play 2, — all-inclusive depending on availability.

Music is for our Fine Dining Room with 23 tables of 2. Genres include Jazz, Easy Listening, Romantic, Blues, Light Rock, Country, or Classical.

Evening play: 6:00 pm – about 11:00 pm (with breaks).

We have a stand-up piano; strings and wind instruments must be your own.

Pre-booking dates can be arranged, but confirmed bookings will only be finalized within 7 days of your play date (depending on availability).
